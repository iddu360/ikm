Background

We are the Christian Born-Again exist to reach out our world with what we have received from the Lord that every race come to the Knowledge of the Truth turn back to the Lord Christ their Savior. INTERNATIONAL KNOWLEDGE COMMUNITY CHURCH, Birthed within INTERNATIONAL KNOWLEDGE MINISTRIES, to cooperate with other Christiandom aiming to fulfill the same Goal of the great Commission and raise up Champion for Christ. We operate in all aspect because the Days are short for Christ return and every servant is to be equipped for the same task.

Aims

Our aim and Objectives as IKCC is to reach out the lost generation with the Gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ through available platforms existing in our generation including Online and media platforms.

  • Preach the Gospel of the Lord Jesus Christ to all classes.
  • Plant and Institutes Churches across the world before the second coming of Our Lord Jesus Christ.
  • To prepare and Send out Missionaries from Uganda across the world In so doing this, we shall startup the Christian Schools from kindergarten to University level. Adult literacy schools, seminars and workshops to various groups.
  • Establish Bible, Sunday School Theological Institutes and Vocational training Centre so as to train all the Church Ministers.
  • Carry social gospel and holistic ministry in Uganda and all over the world.
  • Invest in housing, ware houses dairy farms, transportation of goods and services and passengers, press machinery.
  • To promote true worshipping of the living God, exalt his son Jesus Christ and honor the holy spirit by living exemplary life style that reproduces the character of Christ
  • To promote the unity of believers by encouraging the believers to function within the global fellowship of believers in Jesus Christâ€™ name.
  • Promote peaceful co-existence, conflict resolutions and speak for the voiceless.
  • Advocacy for girl child empowerment and protection.
  • Fight domestic violence by advocating for the biblical norms and practices in marriage.
  • Fight HIV/AIDS and its stigmatization amongst our communities and offer support and advocacy for the affected and infected.
  • Conduct overnight prayers, conferences, prayer and fasting to strengthen the believers and the ministry work.
